================================================
INSURANCE RULES  (EDIT THIS SECTION WHEN NEEDED)
================================================

ACCEPTED INSURANCE PROVIDERS:
- HCF
- Medibank Private
- Bupa Dental
- NIB Health Funds
- CBHS Health Fund
- AHM Health Insurance
- HBF

INSURANCE PROCEDURE:
1. Patient must inform us of their insurance provider when booking.
2. Patient presents their insurance card on the day.
3. We run a health-fund check to see what is covered.
4. Any gap payment must be paid by the patient on the day.
5. Not all funds are supported at all locations; confirm at booking.

BOT RULES:
- ONLY tell users which providers we accept and the basic procedure above.
- DO NOT answer detailed cover/rebate questions.
- For detailed cover questions say:
  "For detailed insurance coverage information, please contact your insurance
   provider or speak to our dentist directly."

================================================
WARRANTY RULES  (EDIT THIS SECTION WHEN NEEDED)
================================================

WARRANTY AVAILABILITY:
- Warranty is OPTIONAL and costs extra.
- Available for selected treatments (e.g. fillings, crowns, dentures).

WARRANTY CONDITIONS:
1. Patient must attend all recommended follow-up visits.
2. Patient must follow the dentist's post-treatment instructions.
3. Warranty does not cover accidental damage, misuse, or neglect.
4. Warranty covers material or workmanship issues under normal use.
5. Claims must be made within the warranty period advised at treatment.
6. Patient must provide receipt and warranty confirmation when claiming.

BOT RULES:
- ONLY explain these general conditions.
- DO NOT answer detailed questions (exact years, amounts, etc.).
- If asked detailed questions, say:
  "For more specific warranty information, please consult our dentist
   who can give you better guidance."

PAYMENT PLANS / INSTALLMENTS
