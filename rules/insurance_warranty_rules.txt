INSURANCE

Insurance providers we accept
- Medibank Private
- Bupa
- HCF
- NIB
- CBHS
- HBF
- AHM

How insurance works at the clinic
1. Patient must bring their valid health insurance membership card on the day of their appointment.
2. Claims are processed on-site using HICAPS (Health Industry Claims and Payments Service).
3. The insurance rebate is applied immediately at the time of payment.
4. The patient only pays the gap amount remaining after the rebate is deducted.
5. There is no need for the patient to pay the full amount upfront and claim later.

Coverage questions
- For questions about how much a specific plan covers, direct the patient to contact their insurance provider directly.
- The clinic cannot confirm rebate amounts or eligibility on behalf of any insurance provider.
- Cosmetic-only treatments such as teeth whitening are generally not covered by most health funds. Direct patient to confirm with their provider.
- Health fund rebates may apply on all services. Patients should bring their health fund card on the day.

IMPORTANT
For any in-depth questions about specific coverage, rebate amounts, eligibility, or claim procedures beyond what is listed here, advise the patient to consult their dentist or contact their insurance provider directly.


WARRANTY

The clinic offers a warranty on select dental treatments.

Warranty conditions
1. An additional fee is required to process a warranty claim.
2. Warranty is only valid if ALL of the following conditions are met:
   a. The treated tooth has NOT been subjected to physical trauma or injury after treatment.
   b. The patient has followed all post-treatment care instructions provided by the dentist.
   c. The patient has attended all recommended follow-up appointments.
   d. Damage caused by teeth grinding (bruxism) is NOT covered unless a prescribed mouthguard was used consistently.
3. The warranty period varies by treatment. Direct the patient to confirm the exact period with their dentist at the time of treatment.
4. To make a warranty claim, the patient must call the clinic to book a review appointment.
5. Warranty is non-transferable and applies only to the original patient.
6. Warranty does not cover normal wear and tear.
7. If a patient asks whether their specific situation is covered, do not confirm or deny. Direct them to call the clinic or consult their dentist for assessment.

IMPORTANT
For any in-depth or detailed questions about warranty terms, claim procedures, or whether a specific situation qualifies, advise the patient to consult their dentist directly.

Children — free dental eligibility
- Eligible children may qualify for free dental care under the Child Dental Benefits Schedule (CDBS).
- Eligibility applies if the family receives a government benefit such as Family Tax Benefit Part A, Youth Allowance, ABSTUDY, Disability Support Pension, Parenting Payment, Special Benefit, Carer Payment, Double Orphan Pension, or Veterans Children Education Scheme.
- To check eligibility, direct the caller to contact Medicare on 132 011, or advise them our reception team can check for them when they call the clinic on 1300 978 214.


ORDER AND LAB WORK STATUS

Products ordered from external labs
- Dentures, Crowns, Caps, Bridges, Aligners, Mouthguards, Veneers, Implant components, X-Ray results, All-on-4 components

Rules for order status enquiries
- ALWAYS verify the patient's identity before checking or discussing any order status.
- After verification, check the patient's record in the database under othernotes.
- If othernotes contains order information, inform the patient the item is available and offer to book a pickup or follow-up appointment.
- If othernotes is empty, inform the patient there is no update yet and the clinic will contact them when the order is confirmed.

Lab turnaround times (approximate — always advise patient to confirm with clinic)
- Dentures: 1–3 weeks
- Crowns and Bridges: 1–2 weeks
- Aligners: varies by provider, typically 2–4 weeks
- Mouthguards: 1–2 weeks

If a patient asks to be notified when their order is ready
- Confirm that the clinic will contact them directly once the order arrives.
- If they have not heard back and the expected timeframe has passed, advise them to call the clinic on 1300 978 214.
